BioExcel Cloud Portal

Centre of Excellence for Computational Biomolecular Research

The BioExcel Cloud Portal has been developed by the Software Development and Operations team at EMBL-EBI to simplify the experience for researchers when running their workloads across different cloud providers.

The portal enables cloud providers and research teams to package and share their BioExcel applications and environments with their respective users. That way, these key applications can be deployed on demand onto the cloud providers the user is able to access.

How to access to the BioExcel Cloud Portal?

The BioExcel Cloud Portal is offered as a service to ELIXIR and other communities. Users will need a freely available ELIXIR identity to access the portal.

Obtain your ELIXIR identity here.

How to use the BioExcel Cloud Portal?

Access to the portal does not provide access to any cloud providers but is compatible with the work within the ELIXIR Compute Platform. Users will need to provide their own credentials to access cloud providers such as OpenStack, Google Compute Engine or Amazon Web Services.

Research groups can use the portal to share appliances and cloud credentials within their teams through the portal if they desire.

Further questions?

Further information on the design and documentation on how to use and contribute appliances to the portal is available. Any questions around the BioExcel Cloud Portal and its use can be sent to ebi[at]ebi.ac.uk.
